Compliance

The KYC AML & CFT standards adopted suo motu by CoinSwitch are closely aligned with regulated/reporting entities under PMLA. Accordingly, we have framed our processes in alignment with various regulations issued by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) and the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). We do not provide access to our platform to users who have not met our strict KYC standards. This has been done to ensure that the CoinSwitch platform is safe and secure and is not used for any illegal activities or transactions, as well as for investor protection and to prevent fraudulent activities within the virtual digital asset ecosystem (VDA).

Consumer Awareness

The company launched ‘Switch’ — an education initiative to help users make sense of modern money. Switch is not a platform for investment advice. It aims to simplify, demystify, and bring clarity to this world of finance so users can navigate the change better. It will bring pre-existing streams of user education, such as YouTube, blogs, and others, on a single platform for easy reference and introduce new streams, including research tools, podcasts, opinion columns, and more, in a phased manner. All information on Switch is free and can be accessed at https://coinswitch.co/switch/. CoinSwitch's ‘Switch Daily’ newsletter is one of the fastest-growing newsletters in the Indian crypto space and provides a daily wrap-up of developments from the crypto and financial space. Furthermore, the app hosts a ‘Crypto Bytes’ section wherein users can view byte-sized news, market updates, and general information from the crypto and financial space.

At CoinSwitch, user protection is of utmost importance. In line with the company’s ongoing product feature upgrades to tighten platform security, the company launched Riskometer, an attempt to help users think twice before investing in a coin. The Riskometer provides a warning on highly volatile coins or when the company feels users need to proceed with caution while investing.
